About me

I came from China and was born in Zhejiang Ningbo. In 2014, I graduated from undergrad school in Shanghai and came to the US. I went to Bentley to get my MSA and graduated in 2016. My first job out of school is an auditor in PwC. After three years, I left PwC and joined EY. At EY, I'm in Transaction Advisory Services team.

However, after being in Accounting for 4 years, I never found myself satisfied. The WLB isn't good in big 4. And if I go to industry, the career potential isn't good, also the job would be boring. So overall, I realized that what this career gave me does not satisfy my lifestyle need. I like skiing, traveling and poker. Click Hobbies for more. I want to be able to work from anywhere in the world, or at least anywhere from the US. I want to have enough time to enjoy my life. I also want to have spend more time with my family, friend and my Furiend.

After some research, I found software engineer is a job that would satisfy my lifestyle -- and pays well:) So here I am, on my new journey to this new career that will give me more flexibility and more potential.
